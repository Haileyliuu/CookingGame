This is a 2 player local multiplayer cooking game! Play with your friends as a cat making sushi or a dog making burgers, 
and race to see who can deliver to your customers the fastest. Complete minigames to cook your dish and serve the meal to the customer. 
Or catch a cockroach and sabatoge your opponent's dish!
